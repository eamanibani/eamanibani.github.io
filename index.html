About

I am a first-year PhD student in Management Science and Operations at London Business School, advised by Prof. Jean Pauphilet.

I earned my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Industrial Engineering at Sharif University of Technology, specializing in Optimization Methods. My research sits at the intersection of operations research and machine learning, with applications in healthcare and sustainable operations. Prior work has appeared in Computers & Operations Research.

About me

I am a PhD student in Management Science and Operations at London Business School (2025–2030, expected). My research is grounded in operations research and focuses on applications in healthcare operations management, supply chain design, route planning, pricing and revenue management, and scheduling.

I am particularly interested in how analytical and optimization methods can support data-driven decision making in complex operational environments, with a special emphasis on sustainability and uncertainty.
